FAQs

What are your office hours?

Our office hours are Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm. During these times we hopefully be able to take your calls however if you are unavailable to call during these times, we would be glad to receive your message via Email or through the form on our contact page.

When can I expect a response to emails or messages?

We always hope to respond as soon as possible. Please expect a response no later than 24 hours. If you do not receive a repsonse after 24 hours, please feel free to send us another email or message as there may have been a problem and we had not received the message.

Can I just arrive to the office without previously contacting?

If we are not busy, although we would be able to hold a meeting when you arrive without previously letting us know we would sincerely appreciate you contacting us before. Once we establish contact, we can set a time and a date which would suit you and which would suit us.

Does a meeting cost anything?

No, it doesn't! We would love to welcome you to our office, free of charge for a consultation.

Do you do personal taxes?

Absolutely! We provide services to people from all different backgrounds. We would be more than happy to help you with your personal taxes and help you in any way we can!

Who designed this website?
