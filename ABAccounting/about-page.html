About Us

Our Story
We were founded in 2013 by Annette Boyle, a certified public accountant who had previously worked in accounting for many years. After working in the industry for other companies, Annette decided to establish her own firm. For her, she was eager to work with people directly and also create relationships with her clients, something which is still paramount today.

We have a vast range of clients, from business owners of various sizes to musicians and videographers. We are keen to work with people from all different backgrounds and we hope we can extend this to you, whether you are a small business owner, a busy freelancer, or someone who just needs advice.
